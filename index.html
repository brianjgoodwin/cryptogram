Cryptogram

Cryptogram - Instructions

In this game, you'll see a quote that has been changed using a secret code, also known as a Cryptogram. The code switches each letter with a different one. Your job is to figure out which letters match up and decode the original quote. When you successfully decode the quote, you win and you can try your luck on a new Cryptogram.

Choosing Letters

To guess a letter, just click on the letter you think is right, and then type the same letter on your keyboard. The game will put your guess in all the right places in your Cryptogram.

If you can't figure out what a letter is, you can use the hint feature. Click on the letter that needs a hint, and press the hint button (the question mark symbol).

You can also reset your guesses by pressing the reset button.
